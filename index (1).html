Cybercrime Rate Comparison

This scatter plot shows the comparison of cybercrime rates in different countries over the years. As you can see from the graph, one interesting fact is that the cybercrime rate in each year is fluctuating, which shows that the cybercrime rate increases with a rate of 40% each year, spiking up the rate in each country present in that year. As you can see, the highest cybercrime rate was present in the year 2012, spiking up to a value of 70% of the total people's population in the country United Kingdom. It shows us that out of the total population of the United Kingdom, which was 63.7 million, 70% of the people were affected by this cybercrime happening around them.
